INSTRUCTIONS

How to Play:

- Controls: Arrow Keys or WASD to move | SPACE or ENTER to talk

Your Character:

- Once you start the game, you will be given the choice to select a character

Objective:

- You are given the task of searching for your Daniel/your Knight (or you can stay single forever >:))
- You are searching for him in a town that you are not familar with and must find clues to where he is waiting for you. You are to locate villagers and find clues to find him. Hurry! He's waiting.
- Quest: Find clues from villagers to locate your knight! (Need 3 clues)

Sound:

- If you prefer to play the game with sound, click the Sound ON/OFF button located on the screen
